version: 1.2.0

# Context

- Mem0 was integrated in 1.0.0 as a replacement for the facts table + ChromaDB
- After testing with real email data:
  - Graph entity extraction quality is poor — default prompts are designed for chatbot personalization, not operational data
  - Internal LLM calls are a black box — no visibility into extraction or conflict resolution prompts/outputs in LangSmith
  - Self-hosted open-source version is rough — bugs, race conditions, sparse documentation
  - Customizing extraction requires fighting the framework rather than working with it
- Decision: remove Mem0 entirely and evaluate alternatives (Graphiti, Cognee, or custom extraction with direct Neo4j)
- Neo4j stays — it's still the right graph database regardless of what sits on top of it
