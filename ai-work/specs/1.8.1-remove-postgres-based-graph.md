# Context

- The Postgres `entities`, `edges`, and `facts` tables are legacy from the pre-Neo4j graph pipeline (v0.6.0–v0.9.x). They stored the old SQLite-then-Postgres graph before Neo4j became the graph of record.
- Every spec since v1.2.3 has noted "DB schema — all Postgres tables stay, including graph tables" as a safety net. That safety net is no longer needed — Neo4j is wired and working, and the graph model is about to change fundamentally (facts as edges, Day nodes).
- These tables have been empty or stale since v1.2.3. Nothing reads from them. Nothing writes to them. They are dead weight in the schema.
- The `entity_types` table was removed in v1.8.0. This continues the cleanup.
